Detailed Description

This class changes the colorspace of the image.

The supported color transforms are:

  • from RGB to CMYK, GRAYSCALE, or YIQ
  • from GRAYSCALE to RGB
  • from CMYK to RGB, RGBK, or YIQK
  • from YIQ to RGB
  • from YIQK to CMYK
